# BOCES IT Service Ticket Analysis

This repo contains the research paper and poster for a semester-long analytics project analyzing IT service desk tickets across two school districts served by BOCES (Boards of Cooperative Educational Services). The project used Power BI to surface operational inefficiencies and deliver actionable recommendations to district stakeholders.

Here are the different components:

- [Research Paper](./BOCES_Group1_Research_Paper.pdf): Full methodology, findings, and policy recommendations
- [Project Poster](./BOCES_Group1_Poster.pdf): Visual summary of key insights presented at final showcase

## Overview

BOCES IT service desks handle support requests across multiple school districts, but inefficiencies in ticket routing, resolution, and staffing were creating bottlenecks and delays. This project analyzed **10,000+ IT service tickets** to identify root causes and recommend targeted improvements.

- Designed and built **7 interactive Power BI dashboards** covering ticket volume trends, resolution time breakdowns, transfer path flows, and technician workload
- Diagnosed systemic issues including **misrouted tickets**, **summer staffing shortages**, and inefficient escalation paths
- Recommended solutions including automated triaging, redirection guides, and flexible staffing plans
- Delivered findings directly to BOCES stakeholders, with insights that informed improvements to service desk workflows
- Served as **team lead** and primary point of contact with district stakeholders and faculty sponsors

## Key Findings

- A significant share of tickets were misrouted on first contact, creating unnecessary transfer chains and extending resolution times
- Summer months showed a pronounced staffing gap relative to ticket volume, contributing to delayed resolutions
- Certain ticket categories were disproportionately escalated despite having straightforward resolutions, suggesting a need for better technician training or knowledge base resources

## Recommendations

- **Automated triaging** to reduce initial misrouting
- **Redirection guides** for common ticket types to minimize escalations
- **Flexible staffing plans** to address seasonal demand fluctuations

## Data

Raw ticket data is not included in this repository as it is restricted to BOCES and its affiliated school districts. Analysis was conducted under a data use agreement with district stakeholders.
